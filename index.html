Game Rules | Read carefully!

1. Game start at the Player 1

2. Press "ROLL DICE" to roll

3. Each time player rolls, the round score appears at the "CURRENT" part and it will added every times player get scores

4. Press "HOLD" to earn player"s round scores, if pressed it will change the turn

5. When player roll dice to 1 point, it will change the turn automatically, means that player lose that round

6. From rule number 5, if player did not press "HOLD" or get 1 score, the turn change and will get nothing that round

7. Game ended when 1 of players reach 100 points
